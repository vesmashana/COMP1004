Password Strength Analysis

Test: How good are you at recognising threats?

This section consists of two elements which assess how likely a hacker would be to successfully 'crack' your password. The first simulates how long it would take a hacker to find your password whilst trying every possible combination. This is called a bruteforce attack. The second will test your password against a database of common passwords to see if yours is in it. This is in reference to a dictionary attack, where hackers attempt to gain access to your account by trying thousands of common passwords. It is important to understand these concepts in conjunction because an attacker could use either one. Get protected - your bank account details could be on the line.
